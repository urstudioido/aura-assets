Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 26, 2026

Studio Ido ("we", "us", or "our") operates the Aura mobile application (the "App"). This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of information when you use our App.

Information We Collect

Data Stored Locally on Your Device

Aura stores the following data locally on your device using AsyncStorage. This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to any server:

  • Bookmarked quotes
  • Notification preferences (enabled/disabled, scheduled time)
  • Premium subscription status
  • App settings and preferences

Advertising

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and use the following information:

  • Device identifiers (Advertising ID)
  • IP address
  • Device and app usage data

For more information about how Google uses data, please visit: Google Privacy Policy.

In-App Purchases

In-app purchases are processed entirely through the Google Play Store. We do not collect or store any payment information. All billing is handled by Google. Please refer to Google's Privacy Policy for details.

No Backend Server

Aura is a fully client-side application. We do not operate any backend servers. All quote data is bundled within the app. No personal data is transmitted to or stored on our servers because we have none.

Notifications

If you enable daily quote notifications, the scheduling is handled entirely on your device. No notification data is sent to external servers.

Children's Privacy

Our App is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The App displays general audience content (inspirational quotes).

Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services that may collect information:

  • Google AdMob - for advertising (Privacy Policy)
  • Google Play Billing - for in-app purchases (Privacy Policy)
  • Sentry - for crash reporting (Privacy Policy)

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
